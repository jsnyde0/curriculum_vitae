That idea evolved into my startup Humainly (https://humainly.com/), where I was co-founder and CEO. We’d sell digital image labelling services to companies in Belgium. Launched Humainly after providing image annotation to clients of our consulting business WebMonks. Humainly combines the best of both worlds in image annotation:
